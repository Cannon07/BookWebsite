Lattice quantum field theories study field theories defined on a
lattice. Lattice quantum field theories have the powerful advantage that
they describe mathematicaly well-defined objects (unlike most standard
quantum field theories), but with the major disadvantage that it is not
know if solutions to a lattice quantum field theory converge to the
correct continuum solution in all cases.
