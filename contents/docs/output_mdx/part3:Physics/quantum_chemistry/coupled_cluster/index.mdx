Coupled cluster methods are a class of numerical methods used to model
many-body quantum mechanical systems. Coupled cluster techniques are
traditionally held up as the \"gold standard\" for modeling complex
quantum mechanical systems. The accuracy of couple cluster methods comes
at the cost of high computational cost.
