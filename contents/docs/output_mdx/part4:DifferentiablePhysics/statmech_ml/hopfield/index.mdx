The Hopfield model and Boltzmann machine are two families of models
inspired by neuroscience and statistical mechanics. These two modes are
in fact mathematically equivalent and are tied to spin-glass models from
statistical mechanics. [@leonelli2021effective],
[@agliari2020generalized], [@agliari2020neural],
[@agliari2020tolerance], [@fachechi2019dreaming]

## Hopfield Network

The Hopfield network has cost function given by $$\begin\{aligned\}
H(\sigma| \xi) &= -\frac\{1\}\{2N\} \sum_\{\mu=1\}^P \sum_\{i,j=1\}^\{N,N\}\xi^\mu_i \xi^\mu_j \sigma_i \sigma_j
\end\{aligned\}$$ Roughly, the Hopfield loss sums over random Ising masks
of the model.
